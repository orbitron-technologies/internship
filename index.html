About Orbitron

Built by engineers who got tired of throwaway software.

Orbitron Technologies started in 2018 as a three-person team fixing other agencies' launch-day disasters. That work taught us the lesson our name is built on: a product that isn't engineered to hold its orbit will eventually fall out of it. Today we're a team of 40+ engineers, designers, and analysts working out of Navi Mumbai with clients across fintech, healthcare, logistics, and retail.

01

Precision

We scope before we estimate and estimate before we commit. Fewer surprises for you, fewer late nights for us.

02

Ownership

Every engineer on your project can explain why a decision was made — not just what was shipped.

03

Craft

Code reviews, documentation, and tests aren't a phase at the end. They're how we work from day one.

04

Longevity

We build for the team that inherits the codebase in three years, not just the demo next Friday.

How an engagement runs

  1. 01

    Discover

    We map the problem, the constraints, and what "done" actually means before writing a line of code.

  2. 02

    Design

    Architecture and interface decisions get reviewed together, so the build phase has no open questions.

  3. 03

    Build

    Weekly demos, a shared backlog, and a staging environment you can poke at from week one.

  4. 04

    Launch & hold orbit

    We stay on for monitoring, fixes, and iteration — launch is the start of the relationship, not the end.

Web Developer

Navi Mumbai · Hybrid · Full-time · 2–4 yrs experience

Apply

You'll build and maintain client-facing web platforms — from marketing sites to internal dashboards — working closely with design and backend engineers.

You'll work on

  • REST & GraphQL integrations
  • Performance and accessibility passes
  • Component libraries shared across projects

What we're looking for

  • Strong HTML, CSS, and modern JavaScript
  • Experience with React or a similar framework
  • Comfort with Git-based workflows

App Developer

Navi Mumbai · Hybrid · Full-time · 2–4 yrs experience

Apply

You'll ship and maintain mobile applications for clients across fintech, healthcare, and retail, from first prototype through app-store release.

You'll work on

  • Cross-platform apps in React Native or Flutter
  • Native module integration (camera, payments, push)
  • App store submission and release cycles
  • Crash monitoring and performance tuning

What we're looking for

  • Shipped at least one app to Play Store or App Store

Python Full Stack Developer

Data Analytics Engineer
